{% trans 'About' %}

{% blocktrans %} Piquant was created as an answer to a series of questions we found ourselves asking over and over again. We wanted to know not only where our food came from, but where its ingredients came from. We wanted to know why exactly salt, fat, and sugar have been considered unhealthy at one time or healthy at another. We wanted to know the rich cultural traditions and history behind dishes we eat every day. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Once we started digging to find some of these answers, we decided to embark on a journey to share this knowledge as widely as possible. Our hope is that you will use as a resource to inform your buying, eating, and lifestyle habits. We also hope you'll strike up similar conversations with people who have interesting stories and lore to share. {% endblocktrans %}
